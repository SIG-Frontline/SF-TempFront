Projects

Advanced Schedule Builder (In-Progress)

The Advanced Schedule Builder extends the features of the current Schedule Builder, with a variety of new improvements:

  • Refreshed UI
  • Course Filtering (Online, Honors, Time, Day, Subject, Building, etc.)
  • Custom Events
  • Prerequisite Validation
  • Calendar Export (& Other Types)
  • Plans
  • and most importantly, D A R K M O D E
We are hoping to have a MVP finished in Summer '24, and potentially propose it to IST to replace the current Schedule Builder.

Room Availability (Not Started)

Show all rooms in NJIT and whether they are in use or not, pulling class data as well as events from HighlanderHub.

This wouldn't cover everything, but it would give a good sense of what's available, especially outside of common meeting spots.
